History of japanese animation

Anime started as early as the late 1910's. Some of the first animators are: Ōten Shimokawa, Jun'ichi Kōuchi, and Seitaro Kitayama. during the 1970's japanese animation really took off and began creating it's own genres. It was at this time that several film makers became famous. Two of the most famous were: Hayao Miyazaki, and Mamoru Oshii. In the 1980's anime became mainstream in japan due to the rise in popularity of certain anime's. There was a big surge in anime production in this time after it became so popular. In the early 2000's Japanese animation films have even earned awards at film festivals, and at the academy awards.
